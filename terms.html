In short

  1. AlpacaLive is a tool for analyzing YOUR data — not a medical advisor
  2. Everything you see is data analysis + information from published research
  3. ALWAYS consult health decisions with your doctor
  4. Your data stays on your phone — we have no access to it
  5. The application is free and open source (AGPL-3.0)
  6. We bear no responsibility for health decisions

Use of the application

  • AlpacaLive is provided "as is" without warranties of any kind
  • You use the application at your own risk
  • You are responsible for the accuracy of data you enter
  • You are responsible for the security of your device and API key

AI Services

  • AI analysis requires an API key from a third-party provider (Anthropic, OpenAI, or Google)
  • You are subject to the terms of service of your chosen AI provider
  • AlpacaLive does not store or transmit your API key — it is stored locally on your device
  • AI responses may contain errors — always verify with your healthcare team

Open Source

  • AlpacaLive is licensed under AGPL-3.0
  • You may use, modify, and distribute the code under the terms of AGPL-3.0
  • If you modify the code and make it available as a service, you must release your changes under the same license
  • Commercial licensing available — contact gravitydesigne@gmail.com

Data

  • All health data is stored locally on your device (IndexedDB)
  • No data is sent to AlpacaLive servers (there are none)
  • Data sent to AI providers is filtered through PII Sanitizer
  • You may export or delete your data at any time
