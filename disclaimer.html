Application Status

AlpacaLive is an open source application (AGPL-3.0) for analyzing health data provided by the user.

AlpacaLive is NOT:

  • A medical device under EU MDR 2017/745 or US FDA regulations
  • A diagnostic tool
  • A substitute for medical, pharmaceutical, or dietary advice
  • Certified clinical decision support software

AlpacaLive IS:

  • A tool for collecting, visualizing, and analyzing user-provided data
  • A source of information based on published scientific literature (with cited sources)
  • A tool to support patient-doctor communication (generating questions, reports)

AI Analysis

The application uses AI language models (Anthropic Claude, OpenAI GPT, Google Gemini) for data analysis.

  • AI analysis may contain errors, inaccuracies, or be outdated
  • Medical imaging analysis (CT, MRI, PET) by AI is NOT a radiological report and cannot replace a physician's assessment
  • Comparisons with reference ranges are informational, not diagnostic
  • AI does not have access to the patient's full medical history — it analyzes only data entered into the application

Pattern-Based Summaries

AlpacaLive shows pattern-based summaries — trends over time, differences from previous cycles, and notable changes in data.

These are NOT predictions of treatment outcomes. They do not tell you what will happen. They show what has happened in your data compared to your own history.
